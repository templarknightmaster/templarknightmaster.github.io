Osmth

OSMTH is the largest Knight Templar Order in the world, and the only Templar Order recognized by a special Consultative Status at the United Nations. In 1999 OSMTH was accredited by the United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC) as a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) with a special Consultative Status, and maintain Missions at the United Nations in New York City, Geneva and Vienna. OSMTH was registered in Geneve (Genf, Ginevra) with the Swiss FRN CH-660.1.972.999.4
Worldwide there are a vast numbers of Templar Knight Groups, but we are not affiliated to anyone of them.
