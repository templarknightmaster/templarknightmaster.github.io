Monthly News

  • Almighty God, who hast in all ages showed thy power and mercy in the miraculous and gracious deliverance of thy Priory, and in the protection of righteous and religious Knights, professing thy holy and eternal truth, from the wicked conspiracies and malicious practices of all the enemies of us. From this unnatural conspiracy, not our merit, but thy mercy; not our foresight, but thy providence delivered us: And therefore, not unto us, O Lord, not unto us; but unto thy Name be ascribed all honour and glory in all places of worship, from generation to generation, through thy Almighty Lord.
