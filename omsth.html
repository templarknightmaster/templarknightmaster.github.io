OMSTH is the largest Knight Templar Order in the world, and the only Templar Order recognized by a special Consultative Status at the United Nations. In 1999 OSMTH was accredited by the United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC) as a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) with a special Consultative Status, and maintain Missions at the United Nations in New York City, Geneva and Vienna.
