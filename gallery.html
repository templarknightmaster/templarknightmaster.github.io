    The Neck Sash for a Knight of the Order of the Templar Lords is a red 8 pointed (Amalfi) Cross with gold border. All four parts of the Cross are held together by a gold Sun and is worn around the neck on a red, purple or white ribbon. It is, together with a sword, awarded to a new Knight at the dubbing ceremony. More Info about our Templar Cross, please refer to History.

  • Passport of the Order of the Templar Lords

    Passport of the Order of the Templar Lords

    Passeport de l'Ordre des Templiers Lords

    Der Passport des Ordens der Welt Tempelherren

    The Passport of the Order of the World Templar Lords is issued for a new Knight and holds information about him as well as dates of occasions of his life within the Order, such as dubbing, participations of ceremonies held and his latest rank and title. This document enables him to identify himself when traveling the world, while meeting other Templar Knights. So far it enabled bearers to one or two free nights’ accommodation at fellow Knights.

    Passport af Orden af World Templar Lords udstedes for en ny Knight og indeholder information om ham , samt dato for lejligheder af sit liv i bekendtgørelsen , såsom dubbing , ceremonier afholdt, og hans seneste rang og titel. Dette dokument gør det muligt for ham at identificere sig , når de rejser i verden, mens møde andre Templar Knights . Hidtil var det muligt med denne passport til en eller to gratis overnatninger på kollega Knights .

    Ang Pasaporte ng Order of the World Templar Lords ay ibinigay para sa isang bagong Knight at humahawak ng impormasyon tungkol sa kanya pati na rin ang mga petsa ng mga okasyon sa kanyang buhay sa loob ng Order, tulad ng taba para sa balat , participations mga seremonya gaganapin at ang kanyang pinakabagong ranggo at pamagat. Ay nagbibigay-daan sa kanya dokumentong ito upang makilala ang kanyang sarili kapag naglalakbay sa buong mundo , habang ang pulong ng iba pang mga Templar Knights. Kaya pamasahe pinagana ito tagapagdala sa isa o dalawang libreng gabi ' accommodation sa kapwa Knights.

    Le passeport de l'Ordre du Monde des Templiers Lords est délivré pour une nouvelle Knight et contient des informations sur lui ainsi que les dates de reprises de sa vie au sein de l'Ordre, comme le doublage, les participations de cérémonies tenues et son dernier rang et le titre. Ce document lui permet de se repérer lorsque vous voyagez dans le monde, tout en respectant les autres Templiers.     Der Passport des Ordens der Welt Tempelherren wird für einem neuen Ritter ausgestellt und beinhaltet Informationen über ihn selbst sowie als auch über Termine von Begebenheiten seines Lebens im Orden, wie zB Ritterschlag, Beteiligungen an Zeremonien und seinen Rang und Titel.     The decoration for a Grand Prior of the Order of the Templar Lords is a red cross with gold border and a gold crown on top. The four red spikes are held together by a gold sun with four golden spikes and is worn on the left or right chest and by some members around the neck on a purple ribbon. It is awarded to a new Grand Prior at a special ceremony with Gala Dinner.

De fire røde pigge holdes sammen af ​​en guld sol med fire guld pigge og bæres på den venstre eller højre brystsiden nongen medlemmer rundt om halsen på en lilla bånd. Denne skærf er tildeles en ny Stor Prior ved en specielt ceremonie med Galamiddag.
